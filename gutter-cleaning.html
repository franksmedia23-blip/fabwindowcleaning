Dangers of Clogged Gutters

Don't let blocked gutters put your property at risk

Structural Damage

Overflowing water can damage brickwork, foundations, and structural timber, leading to costly repairs.

Water Damage

Blocked gutters cause water to overflow into walls, ceilings, and basements, creating damp and mould issues.

Pest Infestations

Stagnant water and debris attract insects, rodents, and birds looking for nesting sites in your gutters.

Winter Damage

Frozen gutters can become heavy with ice, pulling away from the roof and causing significant damage.
