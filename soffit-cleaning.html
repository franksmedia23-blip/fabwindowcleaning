What is Soffit & Fascia?

Understanding these important parts of your home

Soffit

The soffit is the exposed surface underneath the eaves of your roof. It helps ventilate your attic and protects your roof structure from weather damage.

Fascia

The fascia is the vertical board that runs along the edge of your roof. It supports the bottom row of tiles and provides a mounting point for gutters.

Benefits of Professional Soffit Cleaning

Why regular soffit and fascia maintenance is important

Prevent Damage

Removing moss and algae prevents wood rot and protects your roof structure from water damage.

Improve Curb Appeal

Clean soffits and fascia dramatically improve your home's appearance and maintain property value.

Prevent Infestations

Clean surfaces deter birds, insects, and pests from nesting in your eaves and roof spaces.

Maintain Ventilation

Clear ventilation holes ensure proper airflow in your attic, preventing condensation and mold.
